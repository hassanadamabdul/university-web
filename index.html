Hi. Wellcome to Lunabo Apiculture and Agro-Forestry.

Lunabo apiculture and Agro-Forstry is found in the Lunabo River Basin within Western Kenya , In Busia county with the main objective of raising high quality tree seedlings for onward planting to increase forest cover and production of natural honey for local and international consuption .The project targets youth and women for food security and economic empowerment.
